Samsung S21

The Samsung Galaxy S21 features a 6.2" 120Hz Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, Snapdragon 888/Exynos 2100 chip, 8GB RAM, 128/256GB storage, a triple rear camera (12MP Wide, 12MP Ultra-Wide, 64MP Telephoto with 3x Hybrid Zoom), 10MP front camera, 4000mAh battery, IP68 rating, and runs Android 11 (upgradable)

Samsung S22

The Samsung Galaxy S22 features a 6.1" 120Hz Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, a triple rear camera (50MP Wide, 10MP Telephoto, 12MP Ultra-Wide), Exynos 2200/Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 chip, 8GB RAM, 3700mAh battery with 25W charging, and IP68 rating, 128GB or 256GB

Samsung Flip

Samsung Flip specs vary by model, but generally feature a foldable Dynamic AMOLED screen (around 6.7" main, smaller cover), dual rear cameras (often 12MP/50MP), a Snapdragon/Exynos processor, 8GB+ RAM, 256GB+ storage, and smaller batteries, with newer generations adding faster refresh rates (120Hz), improved AI cameras, larger cover screens (Flip 5/6), and 5G/Wi-Fi 6E, running Android with One UI
